# SouqAgent Pay Brand

## Logo

Primary logo assets:

- `public/logo.svg` - horizontal wordmark for decks, README, and landing pages.
- `public/logo-mark.svg` - square app icon, favicon, and compact sidebar mark.

## Concept

The mark combines three product ideas:

- an `S` route for SouqAgent and spend routing;
- a stablecoin-like center point for USDC settlement;
- a yellow settlement star for completed agent payments.

## Palette

- Carbon black: `#090909`
- Stablecoin yellow: `#FFD80D`
- Warm surface: `#FBFBFA`
- Muted copy: `#52524D`

## Usage

Use the square mark for small UI placements and the wordmark when there is enough horizontal space. Keep the mark on a light or black background so the yellow route remains visible.
